About the Foundation

The Colin Farrell Foundation is committed to transforming the lives of individuals and families living with intellectual disability through education, awareness, advocacy, and innovative programs. Inspired by his two sons, Colin Farrell is dedicated to supporting those who the larger systems of governance have neglected.

In 2003, Colin walked with the athletes in the Special Olympics World Summer Games held in Ireland, the largest sporting event of the year. He went on to be a passionate spokesperson, supporter, and advocate for the athletes and the organization.

Our Initiatives

We focus on housing, day programs, workforce support, and advocacy to create lasting change for individuals with intellectual disability. Our initiatives include:
  • Housing and Day Programs
  • Workforce Support for Direct Support Professionals
  • Advocacy for Funding and Policy Change
  • Camp Solas: A Beacon of Hope and Light

Impact

The goal of our programs and initiatives is to make significant strides in improving the lives of those living with intellectual disability. Our work brings hope and tangible support to families in need.
